Papa's Pizzeria Instructions
WARNING: DO NOT SUBMIT SCORE OR GAME WILL NOT WORK
Help Roy run the pizzeria while Papa Louie is gone! You will have to take orders, top and bake the pizzas, cut them to order, and present the finished pies to the waiting customers. Use the mouse as the primary control throughout the game. The four colored buttons in the upper-right will switch between stations (or screens).
